乐记 - iOS APP

  2025-01-25  Project  0 visits

Abstract: Still troubled by a lack of inspiration when writing? Don’t worry. A brand new writing app is coming with a big bang, making your creative journey smooth sailing!

1. Introduction

Do you want to start your writing journey easily but always fail to find inspiration? Don’t worry! This innovative article-writing app is your exclusive creative tool!
Abundant topics to inspire: With over 1000 topics covering various fields such as life, work, and entertainment, you can get creative inspiration anytime and anywhere, easily breaking through creative bottlenecks.

  • Richtext editing for free - style creation: It supports multiple text styles. You can freely adjust the font, font size, and color, and also insert pictures to make your articles vivid and colorful with both text and images.
  • Topic sharing for unlimited creativity: Share topics with others, share the source of creativity, and inspire more creative ideas.
  • Local storage for privacy protection: All articles are stored locally, and you can also set a password lock for your articles to ensure that your personal privacy is not leaked.
  • Widget for convenient operation: Add the widget to the home screen, refresh today’s topics with one click, and start creating anytime and anywhere.
  • iCloud synchronization for worry-free data: Data is automatically synchronized to iCloud. Even if you change devices, you can easily retrieve all your articles without worrying about data loss.
  • Achievement system for fun: Unlock achievements by completing specific tasks, increasing the fun and stickiness of writing, and making your creation more motivated.
  • High-level user experience: Thoughtful designs such as dark mode and haptic feedback bring you a comfortable and convenient writing experience.
